About

AI Thinking is an initiative to foster responsible and safe innovation in the era of Generative AI.

Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies have emerged as transformative tools across a variety of professional fields, including knowledge work, programming, and creative roles. These advanced systems have the potential to augment human skills, streamline processes, and introduce innovative ways of working. However, their rapid adoption and integration into the professional landscape necessitate a focused approach towards preparing students, educators, and young professionals for their innovative, effective, safe and responsible usage. Welcome to AI Thinking!
